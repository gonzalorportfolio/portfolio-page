About Me


I'm a native of Brooklyn, NY, who has been an educator for the past 10 years, working in different areas of education. I transitioned into tech/Computer Science Education 4 years ago based on my love for all things computers. I have worked hard to introduce the world of Computer Science and Tech to students who would not usually have the opportunity. As a result, my students have learned basic web development and grown to understand how the internet works and its social implications.

I need a fast-paced environment where I know my work will have a large-scale impact. I want to be the change in the world I want to see.
